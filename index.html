Making Choices Easier

Wok and Fire has brought its passion for street food. Our Far East inspired restaurant is always filled by the scent of sauce and fresh ingredients simmering on the fire, as we fulfil our aim of providing everyone from vegans to meat lovers with a delicious, healthy stir-fry. We offer authentic Asian street food and delicious stir-frys in an idyllic. Our restaurant delivers the perfect Asian street food from the Far East of Asia to the heart of London. Whether you’re looking for a lunch on the go, an evening dinner with friends, or a quick bite after a late night out, we’ve got something for everyone. Our food flashes from the wok to your plate, and is cooked right before your eyes using only the finest, freshest produce.
